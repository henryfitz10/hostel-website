About Us

Located in the heart of Limerick City, The School House Hostel is ideally situated between Limerick's premier nightlife quarter and King John's Castle. This nouveau establishment is newly opened and offers world class hostel accommodation at affordable prices. We have free secure onsite car parking and an information desk with all the help you need to plan your stay in Limerick City and its treasures.
